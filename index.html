01 / About

About me

I'm a BI analyst working in a regulated environment, where I build Power BI reports, SQL models, Power Apps, and Power Automate workflows for business teams. The work runs from data modeling and DAX to the apps and automations built around the reporting layer.

My work is a mix of technical buildout and stakeholder support. I rebuild models, move logic into SQL, write role-based reporting patterns, automate manual workflows, and help teams agree on KPI definitions they can trust. I'm comfortable on the technical side and in front of stakeholders.

Outside of work, I build data systems on my own. My main project is an AI health coaching system that pulls wearable and nutrition data into a database, connects an LLM to it through an MCP server, and tracks trends through dashboards. I also built a prediction-market analytics project around weather contracts.
