01 / About

About me

I'm a BI analyst at Leonardo DRS, where I build production Power BI reports, SQL pipelines, and Power Platform solutions for a defense manufacturing business. Most of my work replaces manual Excel processes with self-service reporting on scrap, yield, capacity, and WIP.

The job covers a lot of ground. Some weeks I'm redesigning a semantic model or moving logic into SQL views. Other weeks I'm building a Power App to get around a platform limitation, or sitting with engineering and finance to agree on what a KPI should mean. I'm comfortable on the technical side and in front of stakeholders.

Outside of work I build my own tools. The latest two are NOVA, an AI health coach that connects Claude to my WHOOP and nutrition data through an MCP server, and a weather arbitrage scanner that runs paper trading strategies against live prediction markets. Both are deployed on the same DigitalOcean droplet.
